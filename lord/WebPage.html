In the realm of cells, harmony once prevailed. But that peace was shattered when invaders—ruthless pathogens—rose from the shadows, wielding the power of infection and plunging the cellular world into chaos. In response to this dire threat, the twelve great leaders of the eukaryotes united, summoning strength granted by the eternal flame. Together, they repelled the assailants and vanquished the formidable overlord of the pathogens. Yet, this is not their tale. This is the story of their brave descendants—heirs to that legacy—charged with the sacred mission of eradicating the last remnants of the invaders and restoring balance to their world.
